## What the Image Shows

### Product Header Section:
1. **Product Image** (left) - Square image showing product
2. **Product Name** - "Organic Bananas" (right of image)
3. **Category Tag** - "produce" pill badge (below name)
4. **Add to List Button** - Light blue button with plus icon (below category)
5. **Sorting Indicator** - Green icon + "Sorted: Lowest to Highest" text

### Store Cards Section (Vertical Stack):
Each store card shows:
- **Rank Badge** - #1, #2, etc. (top-left)
- **Best Price Badge** - Only on #1 card (top-right, light blue)
- **Store Logo** - Square logo (left side)
- **Store Name** - Next to logo
- **Price** - Large, prominent (below store name)
- **Price Difference** - "+$0.04 more" (only on non-best cards, red text)
- **Shop Now Button** - Full width, gradient button at bottom

### Best Price Card (Costco):
- Light blue gradient background
- Cyan border
- Stands out from other cards

### Regular Cards (Aldi, etc.):
- Dark background (matches app theme)
- Gray border
- Less prominent

### Layout:
- **Vertical stack** (not grid)
- **Full width** store cards
- **4 cards initially** visible
- **"Show more stores"** link below
- **Expandable** to show all stores
