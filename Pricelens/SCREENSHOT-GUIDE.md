## üì± How to Take Screenshots

### Android (Google Play):

1. **On your Android phone:**
   - Open PriceLens app
   - Navigate to key screens:
     - Home screen (categories)
     - Category page (products)
     - Search results
     - Product comparison
     - Profile screen
   - Press **Power + Volume Down** (screenshot)
   - Screenshot saved to gallery

2. **Or use Android Studio:**
   - Open emulator
   - Click camera icon
   - Save screenshot

**Sizes needed:**
- Phone: 16:9 or 9:16 ratio
- Minimum: 320px width
- Recommended: 1080px width

### iOS (App Store):

1. **On iPhone:**
   - Open PriceLens app
   - Navigate to key screens
   - Press **Power + Home** (or **Power + Volume Up** on newer iPhones)
   - Screenshot saved to Photos

2. **Or use Xcode Simulator:**
   - Open simulator
